    What we work on

    The voice is a muscle.
    We train it like one.

    We work cause-and-effect, like a coach in the gym for your voice — find the weakness, give it the right exercise, build the coordination before the power.

    01

    Range

    Reach the high notes without straining, and the low ones without losing tone.

    02

    Your mix voice

    Find the middle gear between chest and head so you can glide through your break.

    03

    Breath & support

    Posture and breathing that hold long phrases steady instead of running out of air.

    04

    Pitch & tone

    Hit it dead-on, every time, and shape the colour of the sound the way you want.

    05

    Kill the cracks

    Remove tension and breaks, especially on the notes that always let you down.

    06

    Style & emotion

    Deliver a song with feeling — riffs, dynamics and the details that make it yours.

    07

    Mic technique

    Sound like the record live, and walk into a recording session knowing what to do.

    08

    Audition prep

    Song choice, nerves and polish for music-school auditions, castings and sessions.

    The approach

    Taught by someone who still does it for real.

    Singing isn't brute force. It's coordination first, then power — like finding the sweet spot instead of swinging as hard as you can.

    i

    Built around you

    No fixed syllabus. Leyla diagnoses your voice in the first session and targets what's actually holding it back.

    ii

    Your sound stays yours

    The rasp, the crack, the grit — if it's part of your style, you keep it. The goal is control, not a different singer.

    iii

    Technique you can feel

    Every exercise has a reason. You'll understand what changed and how to repeat it on your own.
